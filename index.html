a site about me

“I grew up in a small midwestern town in Missouri. When I was in elementary school, I coded some programs, games, and songs. As I grew older my interests began to change, yet I never forgot my experiences and those fun times. My creative spirit led me to the arts and I graduated from college with a Bachelor of Arts degree in sculpture.”

Creativity

“For me, taking a pencil sketch from concept to is fun and exhilarating. I love working with my hands and the creative process. While documenting my artwork, I found that I also enjoyed photography. It was like sculpting on the 2-D plane. Naturally, I engaged myself with many creative projects that encompassed film/digital photography, woodworking and design.”

“An opportunity arose to move to North Carolina to work as the Assistant Director for a Fortune 500 company using my business experience that I developed during my college summers. My focus was working with their proprietary computer software for the Southeastern Division accounts. I found this part of the job was most interesting and rekindled my passion for programming code from my youth.”

Home

“I recently graduated from Tech Talent South’s Full Time Code Immersion Bootcamp in Raleigh, NC. I studied HTML5, CSS3, Ruby on Rails, Domain Modeling, HTTP and RESTful design, Javascript, and AJAX. It was especially exciting to build an app from scratch and to incorporate a Google Map API into it. During the Datapalooza event, I was part of a app team that won "Greatest Potential Impact" and "Crowd Favorite”. My favorite moments at T.T.S. were volunteering for Kids Code Tuesdays as the computer programming instructor. Currently, my code library is growing and am I continuing to learn new languages, methods and practices in the field.”
